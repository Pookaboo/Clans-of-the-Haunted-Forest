Choose your clan, and choose wisely.

Spellclan, a clan of cats with magical abilities and a greenish tint, they live in a old,creepy pine forest.

Wolfclan, a clan of cats that have razor sharp teeth and claws, they mostly have brown, shaggy pelts and live on a rocky hill.

Lostclan, they are lost souls, who are either blind or nearly deaf, with see through areas, they live in a graveyard, but sleep in different tombstobnes. (They dont eat much.)

Biteclan, a clan of vampires that stick to the moonlight when possible, they have no claws, but very sharp teeth, seeing as their vampire cats,they only kill prey for its blood, and they live in a shadowy, foggy forest.
